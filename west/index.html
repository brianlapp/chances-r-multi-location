Our Story

For over four decades, Chances 'R' has been a proud part of Ottawa's dining scene, bringing people together with delicious meals, generous portions, and warm hospitality.

Our Philosophy

At Chances 'R', food is more than just a meal—it's about tradition, family, and community. Our philosophy is simple: to keep improving, growing, and innovating, while staying true to the roots that made us who we are. From our early beginnings to today, our goal has always been to provide the freshest, finest ingredients prepared with care and served with the friendly, welcoming service you've come to know for over 40 years.

Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

No matter the time of day, Chances 'R' is ready to welcome you. Start your morning with our hearty breakfast classics, enjoy satisfying lunch favourites like fresh salads and sandwiches, or settle in for a comforting dinner of grilled meats, pasta, seafood, or house-made specialties. And don't forget to save room for one of our signature desserts, crafted fresh in-house.

Whether you're joining us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, you'll always experience the same commitment to quality, generous portions, and exceptional service that has kept Chances 'R' at the heart of Ottawa's restaurant community for decades.
